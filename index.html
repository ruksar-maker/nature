Nature

Nature is the physical world and all its phenomena, including living organisms, weather, and celestial bodies, that are independent of human creation. It provides the essential resources for all life, such as oxygen, water, and sunlight, and plays a crucial role in regulating the climate and preventing soil erosion. Various natural sciences, like biology and ecology, study the complex and interconnected systems within nature.
